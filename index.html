An evening of heart-channelled music, curated and held
by the witnessing of those who have nestled their way
into my beating heart.

What is this?

Not a funeral.
A becoming.

✦ ✦ ✦

This is my version of a birthday celebration — an ode to the multi-layered beings that we are.

It symbolises the death and memory of what no longer serves me,
as I make space for my choice of becoming.

The concept of this YOUlogy is to curate a space so the unseen and unheard parts of me feel safe enough to display their depths through the art of expression. I intend on inverting the four walls that have heard me cry out — so I can offer permission to all of us to be heard and seen in all of our moving parts.

You are invited as one of those heart nestlers.
